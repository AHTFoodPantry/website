Church of the Ascension Food Pantry at West Park

This food pantry is located at the Ascension Parish Hall at West Park, located at 1585 Rte 9W, West Park, NY 12493.
The food pantry is open to everyone in need. No proof of income or SNAP card is required, anyone who is hungry will be fed.
The food pantry is open most alternate Wednesdays and Saturdays. A sign will be posted out front the day before it is open. The upcoming schedule is:

Holy Trinity Food Pantry at Highland

This food pantry is located at the Holy Trinity Mission Center in Highland, located at 21 Lower Grand Street, Highland, NY 12528.
The food pantry is open to everyone in need. No proof of income or SNAP card is required, anyone who is hungry will be fed.
The food pantry is open Wednesdays and Saturdays. The schedule is:
  • Wednesdays: 10am-12noon and 6pm-8pm
  • Saturdays: 10am-12noon
